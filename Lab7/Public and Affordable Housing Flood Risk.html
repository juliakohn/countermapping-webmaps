Public and Affordable Housing Flood Risk

This map of analyzes the potential correlation between flooded areas in NYC during Hurricane Sandy and the location of buildings categorized as public housing throughout the city. The data used is "Sandy Inundation Zone" and "Map of NYCHA Developments", both sourced from NYC OpenData.
